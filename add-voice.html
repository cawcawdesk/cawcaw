Not every voice needs to be loud. Some simply need to be heard.

Every Caw Begins With Someone Noticing

A crow watches carefully before it calls. CAWCAW believes meaningful voices begin the same way — by observing, questioning and caring.

Share the stories others walk past, the ideas that deserve another thought, and the small observations that reveal something larger about our world.

The world does not change only through louder voices. Sometimes it changes because someone quietly notices.

Your voice can be curious, critical, hopeful, creative or different — but let it be thoughtful, respectful and responsible.
